Kyoto, Japan

The bamboo forest is a large forest of bamboo that stretches very high and is very scenic.

Fushimi Inari is a shrine with a bunch of torii. The torii form tunnels that you can walk through.

Gion is a traditional district with old style buildings. There are lots of shops and restaurants. You can even get dressed in traditional komonos.
